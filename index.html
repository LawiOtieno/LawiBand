About Bluetree Band

Bluetree originated inBelfast's Christian Fellowship Churchin the year 2004. According to lead singer, Aaron Boyd, Bluetree was born out of frustration. "I was frustrated with the idea that worship is only to do with your heart. I don't really agree with that. It's bigger than just your heart attitude towards something. Like the idea that music can be secondary and as long as you love Jesus, that's fine! I don't think that's right."

This is my Favourite Band because they deal in Christian Music. Their songs are motivating people and changing lives around the World.

Members of Bluetree

Aaron Boyd

Aaron Boyd is a Lead Vocalist, Acoustic Guitarist, Songwriter. For the past 15 years, UK worship leader and songwriter Aaron Boyd, has been passionately blending together his life’s motto of Worship and Justice. Loving God and loving people. Known mostly for his chart topping song God of this City that he penned under his previous band name Bluetree, (Dove Award nominated breakthrough artists), Aaron has been influencing the church through story and song, to live out the message of the Gospel.
